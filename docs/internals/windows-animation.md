> Maintenance notes for `windows-animation`. For usage, see the [guide](../guide/windows-animation.md).

- 📁 [Source](https://github.com/microsoft/windows-rs/tree/master/crates/libs/animation)

## How it's built

`src/bindings.rs` is generated by `tool_bindings` from `crates/tools/bindings/src/animation.txt`; `Manager`, `TransitionLibrary`, and `Variable` are hand-written safe wrappers over the UIAnimation COM API.
